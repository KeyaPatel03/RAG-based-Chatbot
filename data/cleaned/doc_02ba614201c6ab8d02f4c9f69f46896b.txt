PRE-LAW

“Pre-Law” is not a discipline.  It is a career intention, and as such, it demands
careful academic planning.  Part of that planning is to choose college courses that
provide a basis for the study of law in a U.S. law school.

At California State University, Bakersfield, three disciplines that are critically
important in law today offer a pre-law concentration in their Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)
degree programs:

Economics: The primary subject of law in a free-market economy
Philosophy: A discipline that specializes in abstract reasoning
Political Science: A social science focused on public authority
Students who plan to attend law school are also encouraged to pursue a Pre-Law minor
in one of these disciplines.
