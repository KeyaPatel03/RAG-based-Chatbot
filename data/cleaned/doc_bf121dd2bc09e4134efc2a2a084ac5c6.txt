COLLEGE OF PROFESSIONAL AND CONTINUING EDUCATION (CPACE)

CSULB offers additional programs for those interested in extended education opportunities through the College of Professional and Continuing Education (CPaCE)--this includes special degree programs, certificate programs, lifestyle courses, and more. There is a different fee structure for CPaCE programs and typically these courses are not eligible for financial aid as they are not state-supported. Prospective students do not need to complete a Cal State Apply application for these programs.

Visit the College of Professional and Continuing Education website to learn more.

ANNUAL SECURITY REPORT (ASR) AND ANNUAL FIRE SAFETY REPORT (AFSR)

Annual Security Report (ASR):
Pursuant to the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act, the updated
Annual Security Report (ASR)
is now available for viewing. The ASR contains the current security and safety-related policy statements, emergency preparedness and evacuation information, crime prevention and Sexual Assault prevention information, and information about drug and alcohol prevention programming. The ASR also contains statistics of Clery crimes for CSULB for this calendar year, based on the preceding three years.

Annual Fire Safety Report (AFSR):
Pursuant to
Higher Education Opportunity Act,
the Annual Fire Safety (ASFR) is now available for view at:
csulb.edu/afsreport
. The purpose of this report is to disclose statistics for fires that occurred within CSULB’s housing facilities for this current year and two calendar years prior. The report also distributes fire safety policies and procedures intended to promote safety on campus.

A paper copy of the ASR and the AFSR are available upon request by contacting the Office of the Clery Director located at the Office of Equity & Compliance, located in Foundation, Suite 160. You may contact Equity & Compliance by emailing
OEC@csulb.edu
or 562-985-8256 (Monday-Friday | 8AM-5PM).
