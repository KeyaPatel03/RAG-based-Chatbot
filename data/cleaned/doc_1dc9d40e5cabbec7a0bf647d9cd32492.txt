HOW TO APPLY TO CSUMB

FIRST YEAR STUDENTS

We’re excited to welcome incoming first year students into the vibrant, life-changing Otter community. Here’s what you need to know to apply to our bachelor’s degree programs.

TRANSFER STUDENTS

Whether you’re coming directly from a California community college or from another four-year institution, we’re excited to welcome you to CSUMB. More than half of our students are transfers, and we’re ready to make you feel at home.

GRADUATE STUDENTS

Advance your potential with an advanced degree. We offer graduate degrees and certificates in a variety of disciplines and formats.

INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

We welcome undergraduate and graduate students from all around the globe to our inspiring central California campus. Learn how to apply as an international student.

TEACHING CREDENTIAL STUDENTS

Prepare to teach at the elementary, middle, or high school level with a teaching credential from CSUMB. Learn more about admissions requirements and the application process.

EXTENDED EDUCATION STUDENTS

Our Extended Education programs span a variety of learning options including online bachelor’s, master’s, and certificate programs, as well as community enrichment courses.
