WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN LOANS, WORK STUDY, GRANTS AND SCHOLARSHIPS?

A loan must be repaid to the lender, plus interest. Income from a work study job is
earned by students hired to work, and it is taxable. Grants and scholarships do not
have to be repaid.
