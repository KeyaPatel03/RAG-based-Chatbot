WELCOME TO THE DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS & STATISTICS!

We pride ourselves on our supportive and inclusive learning community—whether you're here for a course, a major, or for one of our public programs!

You'll find most of what you need on the pages in the navigation list.

• If you consider coming to SSU or checking out your first course in our department, you'll find lots of information on the Advising tab, including a discussion of the kind of active learning you'll experience here.
• Our Degree Programs include pure or applied mathematics, mathematics teaching, statistics, or combinations with other subjects.
• Our Faculty and Staff are dedicated to your success and can help you get where you want to go. You'll find someone with the expertise you're looking for!
• Our Students socialize in clubs, engage in research, and volunteer for community outreach activities. You can find out more about Department efforts beyond our classes on the Activities and Initiatives page.
