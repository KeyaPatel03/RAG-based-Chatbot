Initiatives Overview
Geffen Playhouse
Geffen Playhouse partners with TFT to offer arts education and outreach programs.

TFT students collaborate with acclaimed industry veterans at the top of filmmaking.

Instructional design, ed-tech, and media production services for faculty and TAs, supporting fully online courses.
