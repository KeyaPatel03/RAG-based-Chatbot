BUILDING PATHWAYS TO COLLEGE

At Sonoma State University, we believe every student deserves a clear and supported path to college. Our Guaranteed Admission program, breaks down barriers and ensures students from our partner schools and districts have a direct path and equal access to higher education opportunities.

STRONGER TOGETHER: OUR GROWING PARTNERSHIPS

Through Guaranteed Admission, SSU is proud to collaborate with K-12 school districts and community colleges. These partnerships ensure students receive guidance and a clear roadmap for taking the right courses, and getting the necessary grades to be awarded guaranteed admission to attend Sonoma State University.
