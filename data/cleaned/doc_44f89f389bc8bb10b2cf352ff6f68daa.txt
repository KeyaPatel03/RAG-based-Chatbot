If you are thinking about earning a degree that will provide you with a wide array
of possibilities for a career in mathematics-related fields, then you have come to
the right place.  Our department is an exciting, friendly, and supportive environment.
Faculty members take pride in their excellence in both teaching and research, and
students frequently pursue directed research alongside faculty. You will find a strong
sense of community among our faculty and students;  we invite you to explore the world
of mathematics with us!

OFFERED MATHEMATICS B.S. CONCENTRATIONS

Students who complete a Mathematics program will be prepared for a broad range of
opportunities in business, government, and industries, as well as being prepared to
undertake graduate study in mathematics, statistics, economics, and related disciplines.
We offer programs of undergraduate study leading to the degrees of Bachelor of Science
in Mathematics with four possible concentrations:

B.S. CONCENTRATIONS

APPLIED MATHEMATICS

STATISTICAL DATA SCIENCE

TEACHING MATHEMATICS

PURE/THEORETICAL MATHEMATICS
