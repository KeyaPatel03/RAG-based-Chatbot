ANNOUNCEMENTS:
Financial Aid Offers for Newly Admitted Students
Financial aid offers for newly admitted undergraduate students have begun to be released. Students will receive an email via their campus email address with instructions on how to view their offer.

• Middle Class Scholarship (MCS) Estimates Some students may see an estimated Middle Class Scholarship (MCS) included in their financial aid offer. This is an early estimate to help you plan and may change based on enrollment, eligibility, other aid, and changes in state funding. What to expect: Final MCS award amounts are determined by the California Student Aid Commission (CSAC) and are typically confirmed and disbursed in late September.
