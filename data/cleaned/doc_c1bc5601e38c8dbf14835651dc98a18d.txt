Here to Help Pay for College.

The SJSU Financial Aid and Scholarships Office (FASO) can help you and your family
get the money you need through federal and state grants as well as loans, scholarships,
and work-study programs.

Apply on time!

FAFSA and California Dream Act applications open every year on October 1st. The priority
filing deadline is March 2nd for the 2026-2027 academic year. Be sure to get started
early so you can qualify for financial aid.
Need help completing the financial aid application?  Attend a workshop!
