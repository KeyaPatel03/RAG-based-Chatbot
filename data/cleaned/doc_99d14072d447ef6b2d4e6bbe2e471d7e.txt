OFFICE OF ADMISSIONS

The Office of Admission oversees all applications for undergraduate, international,
and graduate applicants, including those seeking a first or second bachelor’s degree,
teaching credentials, master’s degrees, and doctoral programs.

Our office admits and matriculates those individuals who will strengthen the CSUB
campus community and alumni base. The Office of Admissions is here to help you through
the admissions process when you are ready to get started.

Our mission is to achieve Excellence through high-quality service to students, the
campus community, and the general public, in an environment that fosters individual
respect and promotes growth and success for all students.
