ADMISSIONS

We’re regularly named among U.S. News & World Report’s Top Public Universities in the West. Our historic campus is within walking and biking distance of Chico’s charming, tree-lined downtown. Our graduates go on to become leaders in their careers and communities. But there’s still one thing missing: You.

COST AND AID

Nearly two-thirds of Chico State students receive some kind of financial aid, including an average of over $10k a year in grants and scholarships (free money!). That brings an already-accessible education even closer.

CAMPUS HOUSING

Six residence halls. One apartment building. Over 2,000 beds. And all within walking distance to class, the fitness center, dining halls, and also historic downtown Chico. Living on campus is tough to beat.

CAMPUS VISITS AND EVENTS

Whether you’re interested in our Wildcat Preview open house in the fall, our Choose Chico admitted students day in the spring, or another campus tour any time of the year, we can't wait to show you in person what makes Chico so great.
