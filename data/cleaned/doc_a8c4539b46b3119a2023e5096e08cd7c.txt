MIDDLE CLASS SCHOLARSHIPS

Initial awarding of Middle Class Scholarship (MCS) is almost complete. The California Student Aid Commission (CSAC) requires that we continuously monitor awards for changes to eligibility. Adjustments will be made based on changes to enrollment, cost of attendance or any other factor impacting award calculation. Still missing your award? Please reach out starting in mid-February if your funds are not yet awarded.
